IOT CONFERENCE IN SMX CONVENTION

On the 30th of October 2024, The ICT Students of Grade 11 and 12, as well as College Students whose department aligned with the ICT strand were invited to Mall of Asia to attend the IoT Conference in the SMX Convention, wherein our strand learnt about the future of technology, from entertainment to how it can help the environment.

MOFB PROJECT

Project MOFB, also known as Project "My Own Food Box", started in 2024 as a way to reduce waste by bringing our own lunchbox instead of purchasing lunch at the canteen.
